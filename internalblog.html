Vegan Black Bean & Quinoa Burger

Why You’ll Love This Burger

  • Nutrient-Dense: Made with black beans, quinoa, and a mix of flavorful spices, this burger is full of nutrition.
  • High in Protein & Fiber: Both black beans and quinoa provide plant-based protein and fiber, keeping you full and satisfied.
  • Perfect Texture: Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside—this burger holds up beautifully on a bun.
  • Customizable: Dress it up with your favorite toppings, from avocado and caramelized onions to vegan cheese and sriracha mayo.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • ½ cup cooked quinoa
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs (or oat flour for gluten-free option)
  • ¼ cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed mixed with 3 tbsp water (egg replacer)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for cooking)

Instructions

  1. Mash the Black Beans: In a large bowl, mash black beans with a fork or potato masher until mostly smooth but with some texture remaining.
  2. Mix Ingredients: Add cooked quinoa, breadcrumbs, chopped onion, garlic, flaxseed mixture, and all seasonings. Mix well until combined.
  3. Form Patties: Shape the mixture into 4 equal-sized patties.
  4. Cook: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook each patty for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Assemble & Serve: Place the burger on a toasted bun or lettuce wrap, then top with your favorite condiments and toppings.

Serving Suggestions

  • Classic Style: Serve on a toasted bun with lettuce, tomato, red onion, and vegan mayo.
  • Southwest Twist: Add avocado, salsa, and a drizzle of chipotle sauce.
  • BBQ Lovers: Top with grilled onions, BBQ sauce, and pickles for a smoky, tangy bite.
